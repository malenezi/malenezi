Eight role pathways.
One coherent ladder.

Every Professional-track pathway terminates at one of SDAIA's five professional levels. Lateral bridges connect them, so no learner is locked into a single trajectory.

01 L1 — L5

AI Engineer

مهندس ذكاء اصطناعي

Builds, deploys, and operates AI systems in production. The largest Professional pathway with five sub-tracks: applications, MLOps, fine-tuning, agents, and infrastructure.

30 programs · 5 sub-tracks
02 L2 — L5

AI Researcher

باحث ذكاء اصطناعي

Advances AI capability through original research. Master's entry. Covers ML foundations, deep learning, NLP/LLMs, computer vision, and reinforcement learning at frontier depth.

25 programs · master's entry
03 L1 — L5

Data Engineer

مهندس بيانات

Builds the infrastructure every other role depends on. Lakehouses, streaming, orchestration, and petabyte-scale workloads for sovereign Saudi systems.

27 programs · 4 sub-tracks
04 L1 — L5

Data Scientist

عالم بيانات

Leads end-to-end ML projects from problem framing to production. The bridge between business questions and statistical answers, with portfolio governance at L3+.

22 programs · ML emphasis
05 L1 — L5

Data Analyst

محلل بيانات

Turns raw data into decision-grade narrative. The most common entry point into the Professional track, with bridges into Engineering and Science at L2.

16 programs · BI & analytics

Aligned to the SDAIA
professional ladder.

Five levels, mapped from the official SDAIA framework. Every Professional-tier credential carries metadata for both SDAIA level and SFIA equivalent, so the workforce can be matched to job posts on either standard.

Level 01

Engineer

مهندس

SFIA 3 · Apply

Executes defined work under guidance. Builds, ships, and iterates on assigned components. Typically a recent graduate or career-switcher.

Level 02

Senior

متقدم

SFIA 4 · Enable

Owns scoped problems, mentors juniors, and leads small project teams. Has shipped production work and can review peers' code and designs.

Level 03

Principal

قائد

SFIA 5 · Ensure

Sets technical direction for a domain. Authors architecture, owns multi-team outcomes, and advises leadership on technical strategy.

Level 04

Expert

خبير

SFIA 6 · Initiate

Drives innovation across the organization. Publishes, represents the kingdom in international fora, leads cross-domain research and development.

Level 05

Chief

كبير

SFIA 7 · Strategy

Defines enterprise AI vision. Accountable to the board for AI investment, policy, and national alignment with Vision 2030 priorities.
