About Me

Professor Mamdouh Alenezi is a visionary leader at the forefront of artificial intelligence, software engineering, and technology governance, renowned for driving transformative innovation at the intersection of academia, industry, and institutional strategy. As Director of the AI Academy at Tahakom and a seasoned leader in technology governance, he combines cutting-edge technical expertise with strategic oversight to shape ethical, scalable solutions for global challenges.

In his dual role, Professor Alenezi spearheads Tahakom’s AI Academy, advancing research, education, and industry partnerships in machine learning and AI, while concurrently leading enterprise-scale technology governance initiatives. His work bridges groundbreaking AI applications with robust governance frameworks, ensuring alignment of IT strategy, software quality assurance, and ethical compliance with organizational and societal goals. A transformational advocate for digital futures, he has designed AI-driven curricula, directed large-scale digital transformations, and championed governance models that prioritize security, efficiency, and innovation.

With a career spanning academic, corporate, and institutional leadership—including roles as Chief Information Technology Officer, Dean of Quality Assurance, and Acting Director of Technology Governance—Professor Alenezi excels at turning vision into measurable impact. His research in data mining, cybersecurity, and intelligent systems has secured competitive funding and delivered tools that redefine industry standards, while his governance leadership has optimized enterprise architecture, risk management, and stakeholder collaboration.

A trusted voice in ethical AI and digital policy, Professor Alenezi empowers organizations to harness technology responsibly. His commitment to excellence extends beyond technical domains: through faculty development, student-centered program design, and cross-sector partnerships, he fosters cultures of excellence that elevate learning outcomes and operational performance.

Today, as both an AI innovator and governance architect, Professor Alenezi remains dedicated to building resilient, future-ready institutions. His work continues to redefine how technology transforms societies—equipping learners, industries, and governments to thrive in an era of rapid technological change.
