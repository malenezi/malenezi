About Me

Professor Mamdouh Alenezi is a distinguished professor of software engineering, a prolific researcher, and a visionary leader in technology governance and innovation. His career spans academia, applied research, and strategic leadership, with a focus on artificial intelligence, software quality, and institutional transformation. Renowned for blending academic excellence with real-world application, he consistently delivers meaningful impact across sectors.

Currently, Professor Alenezi serves as Director of the AI Academy at Tahakom, where he leads initiatives at the nexus of education, applied AI, and cross-sector collaboration. He designs and drives forward-thinking programs that integrate machine learning and intelligent systems into scalable, real-world solutions—bridging the critical gap between theory and practice.

His research spans software engineering, data mining, cybersecurity, and intelligent systems, earning him international recognition and competitive grant awards. A recognized authority in digital governance, Professor Alenezi actively shapes conversations around ethical AI, digital policy, and institutional resilience.
