Built on the national standard

Grounded in the National Occupational Standard Framework.

The framework does not invent a parallel standard. Every competency, pathway, and credential traces directly to the NOSF for Data & AI — sixteen occupations defined through expert analysis, validated in three stages, and benchmarked against leading international frameworks.

  • Anchored to NOSF occupation codes and the five-level career ladder
  • Benchmarked against SkillsFuture Singapore, SFIA, and EDISON CF-DS
  • Responsible AI embedded as a gating requirement, never an add-on
  • Designed for Saudi context — PDPL compliance and Arabic/English delivery
