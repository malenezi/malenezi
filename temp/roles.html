The career ladder

One ladder, five rungs.

Every occupation progresses through the same five career levels, each calibrated to a SFIA responsibility level — so progression means the same thing across the whole workforce.

Priority role

A worked example — the AI Engineer.

The AI Engineer occupation (AI-En) is the framework's first fully-developed role. It carries a complete competency matrix of eighteen competencies, a five-stage learning pathway, modular course specifications, and a four-tier credential set.

It is the template: the same five-part architecture extends to every other occupation in the framework, with Data Engineer, Data Scientist, and AI Ethicist as the next priorities.

See the AI Engineer Pathway
AI Engineer at a glance
  • 18 competencies across 3 tiers
  • 5-stage pathway with 5 specialisation sub-tracks
  • Responsible-AI gate before specialisation
  • 4-tier credential set on Open Badges 3.0
  • Mapped to SFIA levels 3 through 7
