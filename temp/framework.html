The hierarchy

Five levels of structure.

The framework descends from broad classifications to the granular detail of an occupation's job card — tasks, skills, knowledge, and abilities.

Tier 01

Classifications

Five broad groupings frame the Data & AI sector by the nature of the work — from business leadership to frontier research.

Tier 02

Specialty Areas

Ten specialty areas branch from the classifications, each a focused field of Data & AI practice.

Tier 03

Occupations

Sixteen occupations — each a recognised national standard with a unique code such as AI-En.

Tier 04

Job Cards

Each occupation carries a job card of key tasks (T), skills (S), knowledge (K), and abilities (A) — the determinants of performance.

Tier 05

Career Ladder

Every occupation progresses through five levels — Engineer, Senior, Principal, Expert, and Chief — mapped to SFIA responsibility levels.
